What we offer

We treat patients with varying causes of oedema, including primary and secondary lymphoedema; lipoedema; segmental overgrowth; and vascular and lymphatic malformations. We treat all age groups including children and provide a super specialist service for patients within the local area and for patients out of area.

We are a consultant led service offering medical appointments Monday - Thursday. Our therapy led service runs Monday - Friday. We are located across three sites (Florence Nightingale Community Hospital; Long Eaton Health Centre; Kings Mill Hospital).

Treatment is given by multidisciplinary specialist lymphoedema therapists, and may include compression bandaging, compression garments, Manual Lymphatic Drainage (MLD), Simple Lymph Drainage (SLD), pneumatic compression pumps and kinesio taping treatment.
