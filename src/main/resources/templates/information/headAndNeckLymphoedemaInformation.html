Head and neck lymphoedema

What is Head and Neck Lymphoedema?

Head and neck lymphoedema is a condition where swelling occurs in the soft tissues of the head and neck due to a malfunctioning lymphatic system. This often arises from treatments like surgery or radiation that impact lymph nodes.
